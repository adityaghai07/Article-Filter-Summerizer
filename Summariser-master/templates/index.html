How does it work?

  • Search your favourite topic

    Name the topic you want to search for in the search bar. The more tags you provide, the better it is for our algorithm to find accurate results. It can be anything, from latest news about our Prime Minister to the annual budget of any year.

  • See the Summary

    After entering the search terms, out internal algorithm works upfront to generate a brief summary for you which can be utilized for quick sum up and brief about the topic, that too in just a few seconds!

  • Read & Enjoy

    Along with the crisp summary you will be getting all the articles related to the searched topics. Just try it once and enjoy reading .
